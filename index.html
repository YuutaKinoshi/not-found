Synopsis

Shakugan no Shana (灼眼のシャナ, literally Shana's Burning Gaze ) is a Japanese light novel series written by Yashichiro Takahashi and illustrated by Noizi Ito. It was published by ASCII Media Works in26 novelsfrom November 2002 to November 2012. The story focuses on Sakai Yuji, an ordinary boy who suddenly finds himself involved in an ancient conflict between the swinging and non-balancing forces of existence. In the process, he befriends a female swing forces fighter and names her “Shana ”. The series incorporates fantasy and slice of life elements into its plot.
